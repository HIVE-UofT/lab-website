Zhenxiao is an MSc student in Health System Research at IHPME, Dalla Lana School of Public Health, University of Toronto. He also works as a clinical research assistant at the University Health Network, where he develops and evaluates large language model to identify social determinants of health from electronic health records and support integrated care for complex patients. His research interests include natural language processing, precision public health, and the use of AI to improve equity and patient experience in hospital-to-home transitions. Outside of research, he is a car enthusiast and recently started playing pickleball!
